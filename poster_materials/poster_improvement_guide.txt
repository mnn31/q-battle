## SPECIFIC IMPROVEMENTS

### **1. Title Section**
- **Current**: "Quantumons: Q-Battle"
- **Improved**: "Quantumons: Q-Battle - Integrating Quantum Mechanics into Interactive Gaming"
- **Font**: 72pt, bold, centered
- **Subtitle**: Authors and institutions in 24pt

### **2. Abstract Section**
- **Use the new formal abstract provided**
- **Font**: 18pt, justified
- **Background**: Light blue or light gray
- **Length**: 150-200 words maximum

### **3. Background Section**
- **Use the new background that connects quantum concepts to game mechanics**

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Don't use bullet points** - Use paragraphs as requested
2. **Don't make it too personal** - Focus on technical achievements
3. **Don't use informal language** - Maintain academic tone
4. **Don't overcrowd** - Leave white space
5. **Don't use small fonts** - Ensure readability from 3 feet away
